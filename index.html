About Me

I'm a soon-to-be graduate of Brigham Young University with a degree in English and a minor in Business, with a focus on professional writing. My journey started with an Associate of Science from Truckee Meadows Community College, and I've spent the years since building experience across social media, content creation, the restaurant industry, and mountain resort life.

I'm deeply passionate about women's rights and world politics, and I believe that words — written carefully and with purpose — have the power to create real change. Whether I'm crafting a social media campaign for an ocean conservation nonprofit or writing a scientific article, I bring curiosity, heart, and a drive to communicate meaningfully.
