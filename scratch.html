Packing Tips & Gear

Whether you're hiking Joshua Tree or hopping trains through Europe, here's what I've learned:

🎒 Always Pack

  • A reusable water bottle
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • A portable charger
  • A small journal
  • Snacks (always snacks)

🧠 Lessons Learned

  • Book trains in Europe in advance
  • Learn 5 words in the local language
  • Leave one day unplanned
  • Eat where locals eat
  • Carry cash everywhere

📍 Top Tips by Region

  • National Parks: get there at sunrise
  • Europe: get a rail pass
  • Beach trips: go on weekdays
  • Desert hikes: start before 8am
  • City trips: walk everywhere
